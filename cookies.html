Smith Consulting Asia Cookie Policy

How does Smith Consulting Asia user cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of text that allows a website or portal to recognize your device and maintain a consistent, cohesive experience throughout multiple sessions. Smith Consulting Asia and its affliated third parties will use cookies to track and monitor some of your activities on and off the portal, and store and access some data about you, your browsing history, and your usage of the portal.

This policy describes how Smith Consulting Asia and its affiliated third parties use cookies both within and outside of the Smith Consulting Asia portal and how you can exercise a greater degree of control over cookies. Please keep in mind that this may affect and lessen your overall experience with our portal.

The following describes how the cookies will be used:

Smith Consulting Asia cookies
  • Enable behavior tracking in our Products and/or Services that is tailored to the activity or preferences of a person visiting our portal
  • Allow potential employers to target specific users (via searches and marketing efforts) who are likely to qualify for a given position
  • Identify individual users to attribute activities such as allowing users to create resume-like listing of user achievements and employment history and save searches in order to more quickly identify newly listed jobs so as to customize the ad experience for our users, including tailoring job and display ads to the technologies a person has previously looked at, the communities a person has visited, and the job ads a person has already seen
  • Support integration of job applications and listings from our portal to affiliated third-party portals for job matching

  • Monitor site traffic and behavior flows of users
  • Collect information on our users' preferences in order to measure the effectiveness of existing products and create more useful products
  • Allow us to track when a user sees or clicks on an ad or later visits a third-party website or purchases a product on a third-party website
  • Maintain the regular business operations of our Advertising and Marketing departments by measuring the effectiveness of off-site marketing campaigns and tactics
  • Allow direct communication between affiliated third parties who hosts a promotional event with us, and users who have opted into the promotion
  • Help us to comply with legal requirements in recruitment and hiring
  • Help to diagnose and correct downtime, bugs, and errors in our code to ensure that our products are operating efficiently
  • Collect impressions and click data for internal reporting and product optimization
Third Party Cookies

The use of cookies, the names of cookies, and other cookies related cookies technology may change over time and Smith Consulting Asia will make all reasonable efforts to notify you by updating our cookies table where material changes occur and from time-to-time.
Please also note that companies and other organization that sponsor pages on Smith Consulting Asia may use cookies or other technologies to learn more about your interest in their products and services and in some cases to tailor such products and services to you.


What information is collected from me via cookies?

In general, we collect most data from you via form submission.
However, there are cases when visiting our site and/or using our platforms in which we may receive certain information through the use of cookies.
This data will generally not include personally identifying information about you.
  • Unique identification tokens
  • User preferences such as favorite tags

How do I restrict cookies?

While cookies are set for varying durations on your device, you can manually delete them at any time.
However, deleting cookies will not prevent the site from setting further cookies on your device unless you adjust the settings to specifically block cookies.

Please refer to your respective browser developer on how to restrict cookies and keep in mind that cookies may be required for certain functionalities, and by blocking these cookies, you may limit your access to certain parts or features of our portal.
